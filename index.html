Our Story

Looking for quality optical experts that you can depend on? Well, look no further.

Day Lewis Opticians has been established since 1991. We have proudly become the leading eye care and eyewear specialists in Kent and the South-East of England.

Each unique practice prides itself on offering the highest levels of personal service and product quality. From the initial consultation, through to choosing the frames or lenses suited to you, Day Lewis Opticians have only one aim: provide the best eye care for all the family.
